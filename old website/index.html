Our Approach

Project Feasibility/Strategic Advisory

 NewEdge consultants perform a comprehensive AS-IS analysis of your landscape to determine the most optimal solution.  We leverage advanced quantitative tools and techniques to analyze your data and system capabilities so we can propose a wide variety of cost/benefit options that meet your needs.      

Requirements Elicitation & Design

Our consultants are experts at facilitating requirements workshops, collecting and documenting business requirements, and ensuring requirements are adequately captured in design.  Whether its creating a formal requirements deliverable via a Waterfall approach, or building and managing a dynamic product backlog as part of an Agile framework, we ensure all stakeholder feedback has been captured and the right level of detail is elicted to to ensure project success.

Build

NewEdge consultants ensure a transparant project framework is in place to maximize collaboration between business and development stakeholders.  We make sure our project plans are mapped to clearly defined requirements and/or user stories, task execution is prioritized based on business value, resources are allocated efficiently, spend is meticulously managed, and project updates are communicated constantly throughout project. 

Testing & Implementation

Our consultants parter with your Quality Assurance team and/or business stakeholders to develop and execute a comprehensive testing and release plan that will minimize project risk and allow your company to deploy with confidence .  We leverage industry leading tools like Jira and Azure to identify, track, and resolve any development defects, and provide detailed testing metrics so your stakeholders have full transparancy regarding build quality throughout the project. 

Change Management & Training

Project delivery inherently introduces some level of change to your organization.  Our consultants employ industry leading frameworks like PROSCI®, and use  tools like MS Visio, to help understand and optimize work flows so companies can seamlessly transition to a new process.  Our experience developing custom training materials, and leading training workshops, ensures end users are prepared for change.
