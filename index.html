Our Approach

Newedge consultants leverage industry leading project methodologies like Agile, Waterfall, and Lean Six Sigma to custom fit project solutions to your needs

1

Project Feasibility

NewEdge consultants perform a comprehensive AS-IS analysis of your landscape to determine the most optimal solution. We leverage advanced quantitative techniques to analyze your data and systems so we can propose a wide variety of cost/benefit options that meet your needs.

2

Requirements Elicitation

Our consultants are experts at facilitating requirements workshops, collecting and documenting business requirements, and ensuring requirements are adequately captured.

3

Design & Development

NewEdge consultants ensure a transparant project framework is in place to maximize collaboration.  Project plans are mapped to clearly defined requirements and/or user stories, task execution is prioritized based on business value, resources are allocated efficiently, and spend is meticulously managed.

4

Testing & Implementation

Our consultants parter with your Quality Assurance team and business stakeholders to develop and execute a comprehensive testing and release plan that will minimize project risk and allow your company to deploy with confidence . We leverage industry leading tools like Jira and Azure to identify, track, and resolve any development defects, and provide detailed testing metrics so your stakeholders have full transparancy.

5

Change Management

Project delivery inherently introduces some level of change to your organization. Our consultants employ industry leading frameworks like PROSCI®, and use tools like MS Visio, to help optimize work flows so companies can seamlessly transition to a new process.

6

Training

NewEdge Consultants have extensive experience developing custom training materials, leading training workshops, and building instructor-led, virtual, and computer based training solutions to ensure your end users are prepared for change.

Our Leadership

NewEdge Consulting, headquarted in Houston TX was co-founded in 2009 by Ling and Erik Sondersted

Ling Sondersted is a graduate from Cornell's computer engineering school, starting her career as a consultant at AT&T, and then later leading projects at Pricewaterhouse Coopers.  After co-founding NewEdge, she has provided advisory and project management services to a number of multinational companies, most notably oil and gas majors like Chevron and Phillips 66.  Ling has extensive experience managing Learning & Development Projects, Change Management efforts, Service Delivery, and executing Scrum and Scaled Agile project frameworks.  She holds a PMP certification, along with Agile Scrum Master, PROSCI, and ITIL certifications. 

Erik Sondersted is a graduate from Carnegie Mellon's Tepper School of Business, starting his career as a trading analyst in the equity markets, and then later as an oil & gas commodities trader at Koch Industries and Citadel.  After serving as an SME for a large scale ETRM implementation, Erik started taking on more project leadership roles, eventually transitioning to a full-time Project Manager leading multi-million dollar projects for global corporations like Conoco Phillips and Centrica.  Over the last 15 years, Erik has led many large-scale digital transformation projects like HR Workday implementations, SAP Hana Migrations, ETRM and ERP projects.  Erik holds a number of industry certifications including the PMP, Agile Scrum Master, Agile Product Owner, and Lean Six Sigma Black Belt.
